ADUJIVITAM


DESCRIPTION:
Goat Days (original title: Malayalam: ആടുജീവിതം Aadujeevitham) is a 2008 Malayalam novel about an 
            abused migrant worker in Saudi Arabia written by Bahrain-based Indian author Benyamin (born Benny Daniel). The novel is based on 
            real-life events and was a best seller in Kerala.
